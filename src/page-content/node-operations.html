Creating a Babylon Wallet


• Enter "Wallets" in "Validators Operations" on the left panel.


• Write what you want in the "Wallet Name" section and click "Create".


• Copy the address of the wallet you set up, located under "Your Wallets" just below.


Babylon Discord: Faucet


• Enter the Babylon Discord.


• Obtain the "Tech" role from the "Get a role" subchannel.


• Go to the "Faucet" subchannel and paste ! faucet [YOUR WALLET ADDRESS]. And enter. Put down your wallet address you copied on Klein, next to ! faucet.


• Wait for TX to complete on Discord.


• Go back to Klein.


• Click on "Wallets" in "Validators Operations" on the left panel again and hover over the wallet you created to see the balance.


Add Validator: Establish your own Validator


• Enter "Create BLS Key" at the bottom of Validator Operations on the left panel and submit after writing your Wallet Name.


• Go to "Add Validators" under Validators Operations on the left panel


• Type any amount you want in the "Amount" Section in "Add validators". For example 50000.


• Enter your Wallet Name.


• In "Moniker Name", write the name you want to give to the validator you want to install. (Attention: Do not write “node101” to your Moniker Name.)


• In the "BLS Public Key" section, paste the BLS Public Key you created in the "Create BLS Key" section.


• Enter whatever you want in the "Fee" section. For example 500.


• Write 0.05 in the Commission Rate section and submit.


Stake: Stake to your validator


• Go to https://explorer.kjnodes.com/babylon-testnet. Enter Staking in BABYLON TESTNET on the top left. Go to Inactive. Find node101. Copy the “Validator Address”.


• Return to Klein and go to the "Delegate" section under Validator Operations on the left panel.


• Paste the Validator Address of yourself you copied into the "Validator Valoper Address" section.


• Write your Wallet Name.


• Set a fee and Amounts. For example, write 1000000 in the Amounts section. Remember, we run the Amounts section decimally in the current beta version.


• Click "Submit", you can copy your TX and view it in explorer. (Attention: Since the Babylon chain works in epochs, it may take a few hours for your TX to appear in the explorer. Do not worry.)


Extra Notes


• Once a day, you can request tokens from the Babylon Discord Faucet to your wallet address.


• From the "Delegate" section in Validators Operations on the left panel of Klein. You can delegate to your own validator.


• You can search for the validator you have installed in the Inactive set by typing your Moniker Name through an explorer such as https://explorer.kjnodes.com/babylon-testnet/staking
